Welcome to Student Housing and Hospitality Services

Our unit represents Student Housing, Food and Dining, McGill Campus Store , oneCard Office, Accommodations and Conferences, and the Faculty Club. We are committed to providing high-quality services that enhance the living and learning experience of our students, staff, and guests.

Student Housing

We offer guaranteed housing for all first-year, undergraduate students and each year, we welcome more than 3000 students in our residences.

McGill Campus Store

The McGill Campus Store offers a vast selection of official McGill-branded merchandise, course materials and general interest books. Our stores are located on the downtown campus at 680 Sherbrooke St west and in the Centennial Centre on the Macdonald Campus. You can also shop online 24/7. oneCard is accepted as a method of payment in store only.

oneCard

Use your oneCard account to buy food and beverages from multiple locations on campus. It's also accepted at the McGill Bookstore for course material, merchandise and everything else in store. It's so convenient!
